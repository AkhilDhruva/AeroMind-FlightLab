Controls & Help

Keyboard or gamepad — both work, and the controller maps to a real transmitter layout.
W / S Pitch fwd / back
A / D Roll left / right
Q / E Yaw left / right
Space / Shift Throttle up / down
H Stabilize / manual
C Camera mode
R Reset to pad
M Mute · ? Help
Gamepad: Left stick = throttle (up/down) & yaw (left/right). Right stick = pitch & roll. Face buttons reset / cycle camera / toggle mode.
Telemetry: Scan the top strip — battery, GPS satellites, signal %, heading, altitude, wind. Low battery or lost signal triggers automatic Return-to-Home back to the launch pad.
